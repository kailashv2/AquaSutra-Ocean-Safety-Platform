üÜò Emergency Response Instructions

1
Assess the Situation
Quickly determine if this is a life-threatening emergency. If someone is unconscious, severely injured, or in immediate danger, proceed to step 2 immediately.
2
Call for Help
Call our emergency hotline immediately: +1 (800) OCEAN-911. If you're in the US, you can also call 911 first, then our hotline for specialized ocean emergency coordination.
3
Provide Clear Information
Give your exact location (GPS coordinates if available), describe the emergency, number of people involved, and current conditions. Stay on the line for instructions.
4
Follow Instructions
Our emergency coordinators will guide you through immediate response actions while dispatching appropriate rescue teams. Do not hang up unless instructed.
5
Assist Rescue Teams
Help rescue teams locate you by using visual signals, staying in contact, and providing updates on the situation as it develops.
