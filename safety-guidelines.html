📋 Comprehensive Safety Guidelines

🏊‍♂️ Before Entering the Water

  • 🔍 Check local conditions, weather forecasts, and any posted warnings or advisories
  • 👥 Never swim alone - always use the buddy system for safety
  • 🏖️ Choose lifeguarded beaches when possible and swim in designated areas
  • 💪 Assess your swimming ability honestly and stay within your limits

🌊 Understanding Ocean Hazards

  • 🌀 Rip currents: Look for channels of churning, discolored water moving seaward
  • 🌊 Large waves: Can knock you down and cause injuries - respect wave power
  • 🐠 Marine life: Be aware of local wildlife and maintain respectful distance
  • 🪨 Underwater hazards: Watch for rocks, reefs, and sudden depth changes

🚤 Boating Best Practices

  • 🦺 Ensure all passengers wear properly fitted life jackets at all times
  • 📋 File a float plan with departure/return times and intended route
  • 📡 Carry VHF radio, flares, and other required safety equipment
  • ⛽ Check fuel levels, engine condition, and weather before departure

🆘 Emergency Response

  • 📞 Call 911 immediately for any life-threatening emergency
  • 🛟 If someone is drowning: Throw, don't go - use flotation devices
  • 🫁 Learn CPR and basic first aid - these skills save lives
  • 📍 Know your exact location to provide to emergency responders
