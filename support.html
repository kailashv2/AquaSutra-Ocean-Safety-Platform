Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report an ocean hazard? ‚ñº
You can report ocean hazards through our dedicated reporting interface. Click "Report Hazard" on the homepage or visit our hazard reporting page. For life-threatening emergencies, always call 911 first.
How do I access real-time monitoring data? ‚ñº
Real-time monitoring data is available through our Ocean Monitoring dashboard. You can also access data via our API with proper authentication. Visit our API documentation for integration details.
What should I do in an ocean emergency? ‚ñº
For immediate life-threatening emergencies, call 911 first. Then use our Emergency Response Center to coordinate with local authorities and access additional resources. Our emergency hotline is available 24/7.
How do I integrate with government systems? ‚ñº
Government agencies can access our dedicated Government Portal for secure data sharing and integration. Contact our support team for access credentials and integration assistance.
Is there training available for the platform? ‚ñº
Yes! We offer comprehensive training through our Resources section, including safety guidelines, training materials, and certification programs. Join our Community Hub for additional learning opportunities.
